# User & Admin Manual: Smartifly Xtream-UI

Guide for administrators and end-users of the Smartifly Xtream-UI platform.

## 1. Admin Panel Guide (For Administrators)
Manage the ecosystem from the Xtream-UI Admin Panel.

### App & License Management
- **Apps**: View and manage the list of applications connected to the system.
- **Licenses**: Generate and manage license keys for users.
- **System Broadcasts**: Send notifications that appear directly on the TV/Mobile app users' screens.

### Security & Compliance
- **Banned IPs**: Monitor and block IPs that violate terms or attempt unauthorized access.
- **Audit Logs**: Review a history of all administrative actions for accountability.

---

## 2. Smartifly App Guide (For Users)
The app is optimized for TV and Mobile experiences.

### Navigation (TV)
- Use the **D-Pad (Arrows)** on your remote to navigate menu items.
- Focus is indicated by a highlighted border or color change.
- Press **OK/Select** to enter a section.

### Features
- **Dashboard**: Access your core apps and services.
- **Notifications**: View system-wide broadcasts from the administrator.
- **Settings**: Manage your local profile and check connection status.

---

## 3. Customer Portal (For End Users)
Users can manage their accounts via the web-based Customer Portal.

- **Account Management**: Update profile details.
- **Subscriptions**: View active services and renewal dates.
- **Licensing**: Retrieve active license keys for app activation.

---

## 4. Troubleshooting
- **Login Issues**: Ensure you have an active license assigned to your device.
- **Connection Errors**: Check your internet stability and verify the API endpoint is reachable.
- **App Crash**: Clear app cache or reinstall from the official distribution link.
